Rakdos: in his own words...

I’m Rakdos. Two years old, blazing orange fur, and a tail with a mind of its own. I wasn’t born to blend in—I was born to rule. I dominate windowsills, scale cabinets, and attack shadows for sport. Toys? I prefer keys and anything fragile. I nap like a king—on laptops, clean laundry, or directly in your way. They named me after a demon, but I’ve got charm to match the chaos. I’ll headbutt your face and steal your food in the same breath. And when I purr? You’ll forgive everything.
